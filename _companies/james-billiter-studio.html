He started Billiter Studio to pursue his love for custom typography, pattern and illustration. Inspired by Art History, Billiter Studio takes an “applied art” approach to design: once a concept is developed for an audience, Billiter brings these ideas to life through hand-crafted methods. Billiter uses his experience in printmaking, sign painting, ceramics and sculpture to capture his interests in subjects such as architecture, nature, cycling, music, and beer brewing.
