Fibonacci Brewing Company is a nanobrewery and urban farm with an onsite Airbnb. We choose to source locally for the majority of our beers through local farms and ingredients grown onsite and foraged nearby. We are also a winery and offer a Vidal Blanc and Cabernet by the glass and the bottle.

We offer a local farmers market on the first Sunday of the month May through November. We also offer Fib Farm workshops which include topics like: brewing kombucha, herbal gardens, composting, native bees and pollination, and more.
