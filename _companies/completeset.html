CompleteSet is the exclusive, invite-only community that connects collectors to each other and the brands they love. CompleteSet provides a massive, collector-curated database of brand-centric collectibles. Collectors from around the world use the service to build a personalized digital catalog of their most prized possessions. Their catalogs are then monitored in real-time to automate the tedious search for the items they want and need to complete their collections.
