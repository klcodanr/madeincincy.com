Headquartered in Cincinnati, SparkPeople.com is a multi-platform media and technology company with a network of leading health, fitness, and food websites. Its mission is to SPARK millions of PEOPLE to reach their goals. SparkPeople is employee-owned, with one outside minority investor, AOL Founder Steve Case. According to comScore, SparkPeople.com was America's largest weight loss website in 2011. SparkPeople has over 13.5 million registered members in more than 100 countries, and regularly generates over 85 million pageviews, 9.5 million unique visitors, and 16.7 million total visits per month.
