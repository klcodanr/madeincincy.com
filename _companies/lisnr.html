LISNR is a high frequency, inaudible technology; a communication protocol that sends data over audio. We use inaudible sound waves called Smart Tones™ to transmit information.

LISNR transmits customizable packets of data every second that enable proximity data transmission, second-screen functionality, authentication and low-fi device to device connectivity on any LISNR-enabled device.
