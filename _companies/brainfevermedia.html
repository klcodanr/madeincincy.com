BrainFeverMedia creates image editing applications for mobile and desktop; LensFlare, LensLight, and AlienSky for iOS, and LensFlare Studio for Mac. There are professional tools to create ultra-realistic lighting and optical effects for photography.

LensFlare for iOS has been featured in Wired Magazine. LensFlares has been ranked the top selling Photo app in the Mac App Store.
