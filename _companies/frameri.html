Frameri makes glasses with a single lens shape that fits multiple boutique frames, a simple idea executed to the highest quality. Made for all walks of life, the explorers and adventurers, behind the monitor or in front of the lens, glasses for people who need them. No gimmicks or speeches, just a simple idea. One set of lenses, fitting multiple frames.

Frameri has a shop of their own right in the heart of OTR at 1419 Vine St. Also Frameri can be found in 13 Wing Eyewear locations in the Tri-State area.
