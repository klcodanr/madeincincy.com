Girl Develop It (GDI) is a low-cost education program that enables underrepresented groups, with a focus on women, to learn and practice software development in a supportive environment. Hosted at Brandery beginning July 12, 2012.

Unless noted, all classes assume no technical background. Beginners are welcome and encouraged. The courses also serve as excellent refreshers for those currently in the tech industry.
