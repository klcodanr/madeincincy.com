Join our group of entrepreneurs committed to building a profitable business in Cincinnati, OH. Once a month we gather as like-minded entrepreneurs to be inspired, get educated and equipped. Our group exists to support each other as we strive to build businesses that create local jobs, generate wealth and lift our economy.

For our group launch we'll be joined by Derick Thompson author, angel investor and founder of StartupRunner. Together we'll discuss how to build a profitable business in Cincinnati, OH.

View the group guide and access resources at https://startuprunner.com/events/cincinnati/
