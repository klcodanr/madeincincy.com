On October 6, 2016 starting at 4:30 p.m., UPSTART, a street party designed to inspire the entrepreneur in all of us will take place in Covington for the fourth year in a row. This year the event will be held at 632 Russell St. Covington, KY in the alley between Washington Avenue and Russell Street.

UPSTART mixes dozens of groups, leaders, and organizations equipped to facilitate the transition from dream to reality.

Join small business owners, lenders, advisers, and many others in a street festival to celebrate entrepreneurship and get inspiration and advice from existing companies. Local live music, beer, and food will be available to offer a fun atmosphere to mingle with other passionate entrepreneurs.

UPSTART is sponsored by NKY Chamber of Commerce, NKY Tri-County Economic Development Corporation (Tri-ED), and Kentucky Innovation Network at Northern Kentucky. To become a sponsor email Sheryl Stolze at sheryl@nkyinnovation.org.
